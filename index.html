Our Location: Clonmel

Clonmel Town is on the main route between Waterford and Cork or Limerick and is a mere 1 hour 45 minutes from Dublin thanks to the M8 and M9 motorways.

Freeman Motorcycles and Freeman Motors are located on the main thoroughfare entering Clonmel Town.

Situated between two busy main auto dealerships, three large supermarkets and walking distance to Clonmel's main Shopping Centre.
